SCHEDULE ME

FINDING TIME MADE EASY

The Problem

According to the British Broadcasting Corporation's Loneliness Experiment, younger people (ages 16-24) are found to have the highest rate of loneliness among all age groups (Hammond). Regardless of demographics and age, loneliness can lead to negative physical and psychological effects, such as increased stress and a weakened immune and cardiovascular system (Hawkley).

There never seems to be enough hours throughout the day. Whether you’re an introvert or an extrovert, we’ll all eventually want to hang out and catch up with old friends or step outside and meet new people. Finding the time and making it happen, however, is much harder than it seems. Not everyone is in the same area — especially those that are away from home for school — and everyone has different schedules, interests, and needs. Making everything work for a day or just a couple of hours could take weeks of planning. There may be solutions for this problem in the professional workplace, but a more personal one is needed.

The Solution

Sharing one’s calendar, texting back and forth, and having spontaneous trips are simple solutions. ScheduleMe, however, will be able to automate finding the best suitable time for everyone! Users will be able to share their “free-time” amongst friends; they’ll be able to easily see when their friends are all available. With a simple click of a button, a recommended time that fits everyone’s schedule will appear. Friend groups will no longer have to repeatedly ask and keep track of when everyone is free. Once a time is chosen, the event will then be added to everyone’s respective Google Calendar. Scheduling will be at the tip of everyone’s fingers!
